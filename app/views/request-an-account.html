To get a Register trainee teachers (Register) account you need a DfE Sign-in account.

Get a DfE Sign-in account if you do not already have one.

Then request an account for Register by emailing becomingateacher@digital.education.gov.uk

The email address used for your DfE Sign-in and Register account must:

  • be a named email address, for example jane.doe@example.com
  • have an email domain from your organisation, for example we cannot accept emails that end with @gmail.com or @icloud.com

In your email, tell us:

  • the name of your training provider or Lead school
  • that you would like to start using Register
  • the email address used for your DfE Sign-in account

We will email you once we have set up your account.
